Wenjia Xu

PhD candidate

University of Chinese Academy of Sciences

Currently, I'm doing research on Explainable Artificail Inteligience with Prof. Zeynep Akata and Prof. Bernt Schiele at Max Plank Institute of Informatics.

I am a PhD student at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Yirong Wu. My main area of interest is explaining the behavior of neural networks.

My formal background is in Electrical Engineering (B.Sc. hons. 2016 at Beijing Institute of Technology). During Jan.2016 - Jul.2016, I finished my bachelor thesis on ''Evaluation of Privacy-Preserving Methods for Smart Grids'' under the supervision of Prof. Tobias Oechtering in KTH.
