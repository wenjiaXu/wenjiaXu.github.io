PhD candidate

University of Chinese Academy of Sciences

I am a fourth-year PhD student at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Yirong Wu. My main area of interest is explaining the behavior of neural networks.

Currently, I'm doing research on Explainable Artificail Inteligience under the supervision of Prof. Zeynep Akata at Max Plank Institute of Informatics.

My formal background is in Electrical Engineering (B.Sc. hons. 2014 at Beijing Institute of Technology). During Jan.2016 - Jul.2016, I finished my bachelor thesis on ''Evaluation of Privacy-Preserving Methods for Smart Grids'' under the supervision of Prof. Tobias Oechtering in kth.
